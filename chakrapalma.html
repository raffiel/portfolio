Chakra Palma Project

I had the opportunity to develop a captivating branding design for a coconut fiber manufacturer startup. This involved creating a visually appealing logo that represents the essence of the company's identity. Additionally, I designed compelling promotional materials that effectively communicate the unique value proposition of the products. To further showcase the brand's potential, I crafted a visually stunning marketing presentation that visually encapsulates the company's vision and mission. These design efforts were completed within a one-week timeframe, ensuring a seamless integration of the brand's identity across various marketing channels.

Using my expertise in design and leveraging the power of Photoshop, I meticulously crafted the logo design, ensuring it visually captures the essence of the coconut fiber manufacturer.
