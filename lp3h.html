Social Media Design

LP3H Hidayatullah Project

I designed and created social media content, including posts for special occasions, event updates, and other relevant content for the institution. The content featured two formats: 1:1 feeds and 9:16 stories. Utilizing Photoshop, I produced engaging and responsive designs for various social media platforms.

Throughout the content design process, I carefully represented the institution's identity through color selection and ornaments. The green color symbolized freshness and sustainability, reflecting the institution's commitment to supporting the halal industry. Additionally, the yellow color conveyed cheerfulness and warmth, supporting clients in obtaining their desired halal certifications. The integration of Arabic/Islamic ornaments added an aesthetic touch while aligning with the institution's vision of assisting food and beverage producers in meeting halal certification requirements.
