UP in Cafe Project

By creating a comprehensive branding design for a cafe, including logo design, menu book, social media content, and other promotional materials, I successfully established its distinctive visual identity. The strategic implementation of these designs led to a remarkable surge in footfall, attracting a significant influx of visitors within the initial two weeks of the cafe's grand opening.

By utilizing my expertise in design and leveraging the power of Photoshop, I meticulously crafted each element of the branding design for the cafe. Through skillful composition and thoughtful selection of fonts and colors, I designed an appealing menu book that entices customers to explore the culinary offerings.
